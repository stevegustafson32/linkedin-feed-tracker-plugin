## Why This Matters

Without LFT, you're guessing what to post based on what you remember seeing in your feed. LinkedIn's algorithm shows you maybe 5% of what your network actually posts.

With LFT, you see everything. Every post from every connection, every night. And instead of raw data, Claude tells you what it means and what to do about it.

The difference: posting based on data vs. posting based on vibes.
